What this practice does
Ho'oponopono works on the belief that everything we experience is a projection of our own memory and data. By saying these 4 phrases, you're not apologising to others — you're cleaning the data inside you that created the experience. The phrases work even if you say them silently. Repeat until you feel a release or softening.
Direct it to:
Morning: anyone you're carrying tension with — Susan, situations, yourself.

Evening: the whole day — every moment you reacted, judged, or held on.

Anytime: when you feel stuck, reactive, resentful, or heavy.
